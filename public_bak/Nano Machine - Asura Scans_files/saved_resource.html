                                                                                      So I started re-reading this after 2 years because I ran out of anything to read and I'll do a quick review because I'm waiting for a new chapter release and started reading the novel again:

                                                                                      REALLY WILD SPOILERS!! DONT READ IF YOU DON'T WANT TO RUIN THE FUN

                                                                                      First of all, when I started reading this, i stopped early as hell in 2021-22? (latest chapter at the time) and fell in love with it and proceeded to read both novels.

                                                                                      There are no differences between the Manhwa and the novel, even the chapter names are the same which makes it really easy to transition to novel if you wanna read it.

                                                                                      The art got REALLY good mid 60ish chapters so if you start reading and see that the art is kinda bland, don't worry about it.

                                                                                      Even though the plot is "generic" (lol), it stays true to itself and the thinking behind it is done really deeply, generally nothing happens randomly and has a purpose and history behind it

                                                                                      What you won't see in this manhwa until the last chapters (which is in like 50-60ish chapters at the time of me writing this is the time travel concept is written really good and has substance and an actual story behind it, which made me really enjoy it

                                                                                      The power-ups are satisfactory and fast, I really like how the MC is ignorant and is a true "Heavenly Demon" , you don't need to read like 300 chapters to see the MC attain decent enough power to go against his foes in HIS time, he does it pretty quickly and gets his revenge for his mother really early.

                                                                                      The main enemy is the Blade God and his clan, which is mentioned in chapter 81 of the Manhwa but he hasn't appeared personally yet.

                                                                                      Blade God is a transcendent being that has lived for very long time and still is living. He travels through time. He fought with Cheon Ma, the 1st Heavenly Demon and the strongest until Yeo Woo passes him much later, and centuries later he takes the arm of Sword Demon.

                                                                                      THIS IS A REALLY IMPORTANT SPOILER AND SPOILS EVERYTHING SO READ IT IF YOU DON'T CARE:

                                                                                      In the original time-axis, Blade God' was Cheon Yeo Woon's subordinate and Cheon Yeo Woon in that time-axis was born in a peaceful atmosphere and had conquered 'Murim' and had become 'Heavenly Master' with the help of 5 Spirit Beast Cores and gained Immortality.
                                                                                      Blade God even after gaining Immortality couldn't defeat 'Cheon Yeo Woon' of that (original) timeline and instead time-travelled into the past in different time-periods and created multiple different 'time-axis' stopping Cheon Yeo Woon from being 'born' or gaining 'Immortality' in those 'time-axis' and conquering those 'time-axis' with his 'Blade Six' Group, one such 'time-axis' is the one 'Cheon Mu-Sung' is from

                                                                                      Blade God mentioned that he had fought a different future version of 'Cheon Yeo Woon' who was called Demon God and that the present one has surpassed him.

                                                                                      What made me fall in love with this Manhwa is the things I haven't seen in other manhwas, such as the MC bitching the emperor of china, meddling in other lands, being a savage and holding the world in his palms and being really, really op without any roadblocks or the enemies having plot armor. He doesn't care who he's up against and always puts himself, his people and clan first.

                                                                                      And is a quick, satisfactory read.

                                                                                      ANYWAYS, to keep it short, WHAT YOU'RE SEEING IN THIS MANHWA IS INTRODUCTORY AND JUST THE START so I wanted to share it somewhat to keep you amped up because I really love this manhwa and the story!

                                                                                      TLDR

                                                                                      Much more to come so you must wait but it'll be worth it lol, the plot is kind of a mind-fuck and involves different TIMELINES.

                                                                                                                Decent series for someone who's starting to read manhwa, but it's not nearly as good as everyone makes it out to be. This is definitely an overglazed series, much like how people viewed Solo Leveling back then.

                                                                                                                I give it a 6/10, it's clearly the author's weaker series between Myst, Might, Mayhem and Absolute Sword Sense. It's not a bad score, but for me, this series is just decent, border-lining the mid territory.

                                                                                                                Nano Machine knows what to do in the sense of giving the reader what they want, a dopamine rush from how the MC is just some overpowered guy who does everything by himself and looks awesome while doing anything. The arcs are good, but only because of that rush of seeing the MC do some cool shit and be a bit "edgy".

                                                                                                                The story couldn't be simpler: weak guy receives a chance to be strong, and that's about it. His enemies are always beyond idiotic and complacent, and his allies are always worse than him, lacking the personality and weight they once had. It really infuriates me how the series "killed off" the side characters, they're just husks of their former selves.

                                                                                                                If the characters had received better treatment throughout the series, both enemies and allies, the side characters werent erased, and if Nano didn't just vanish from the story as a character, then it would be an easy 7/10.
